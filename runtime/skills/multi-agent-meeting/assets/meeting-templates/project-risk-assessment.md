# Project Risk Assessment Meeting Template

## Meeting Type
Project risk assessment meeting

## When to Use
- New project initiation assessment
- Project phase risk review
- Project change impact assessment
- Project termination decision
- Project priority ranking

## Recommended Participants

### Minimum (3 agents)
1. **Project manager**: Assess from a project management perspective
2. **Relevant engineer** (Architect/Backend/Frontend): Assess technical feasibility risk
3. **Financial advisor**: Assess cost and budget risk

### Full (5 agents)
1. **Project manager**: Schedule, resource, risk management
2. **Architect**: Technical implementation and architecture risk
3. **Product manager**: Business value impact assessment
4. **Market analyst**: Market and competitive risk
5. **Financial advisor**: Cost and budget risk

**Chair**: Executive assistant

## Typical Discussion Points

### Technical Risks
- Technology selection and architecture risk
- Team capability and experience gaps
- Third-party dependency risk
- Tech debt accumulation

### Operational Risks
- User adoption and market risk
- Operational capability and resource constraints
- Data security and privacy risk
- Regulatory policy changes

### Project Risks
- Schedule delay risk
- Cost overrun risk
- Team attrition risk
- Frequent requirement changes

### Business Risks
- Competitor actions
- Market demand shifts
- Revenue shortfall
- Low ROI

## Risk Assessment Dimensions

| Dimension           | Evaluation Criteria                                           |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Likelihood**      | Very low / Low / Medium / High / Very high                    |
| **Impact**          | Minimal / Minor / Medium / Major / Critical                   |
| **Detectability**   | Easy to detect / Hard to detect in time                       |
| **Controllability** | Fully controllable / Partially controllable / Hard to control |

## Typical Decision Outcomes

- **Green light**: Risks are manageable, recommend proceeding
- **Yellow light**: Need risk mitigation measures before proceeding
- **Red light**: Risks too high, recommend delaying or canceling
- **Adjust**: Need to modify scope or objectives before reassessment
- **Phased**: Implement in phases to reduce overall risk

## Example Meeting Topics
- New project risk assessment
- Core system refactoring feasibility assessment
- Overseas market expansion risk assessment
- Large-scale platform build risk assessment
- AI product development risk assessment
