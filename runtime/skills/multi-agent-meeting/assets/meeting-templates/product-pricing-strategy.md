# Product Pricing Strategy Meeting Template

## Meeting Type
Product pricing strategy meeting

## When to Use
- New product pricing
- Product price adjustments
- Competitive pricing strategy
- Subscription model design
- Promotional pricing

## Recommended Participants

### Minimum (3 agents)
1. **Market analyst**: Provide market and competitive data
2. **Product manager**: Assess product value and user needs
3. **Financial advisor**: Cost and revenue analysis

### Full (4 agents)
1. **Market analyst**: Market research and competitive analysis
2. **Product manager**: Product planning and value proposition
3. **Financial advisor**: Cost accounting and profit analysis
4. **Project manager**: Resource and timeline assessment

**Chair**: Executive assistant

## Typical Discussion Points

### Market Analysis
- Competitor price comparison
- Market price range
- User price sensitivity
- Target customer segment positioning

### Product Value
- Product uniqueness and differentiation
- User perceived value
- Brand premium capacity
- Substitute threats

### Cost Structure
- Product development costs
- Production and service costs
- Marketing and sales costs
- Expected profit margin

### Strategy Options
- **Penetration pricing**: Low price to capture market share quickly
- **Skimming pricing**: High price targeting premium segments
- **Value-based pricing**: Price based on perceived value
- **Competitive pricing**: Match or beat competitors

## Typical Decision Outcomes

- **Fixed pricing**: Set a specific price or price range
- **Tiered pricing**: Offer multiple versions at different price points
- **Subscription model**: Design monthly/annual plans
- **Dynamic pricing**: Adjust flexibly based on conditions
- **Free trial + conversion**: Offer a trial period then convert to paid

## Example Meeting Topics
- SaaS product subscription pricing plan design
- New smart hardware pricing strategy
- Enterprise software version pricing and discount strategy
- E-commerce promotion pricing strategy
- Freemium model paid conversion point design
